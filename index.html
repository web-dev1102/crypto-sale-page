About

Kouta-Kun Inu

Kouta-Kun Inu is a community and like minded driven project. Over the years and  recent times, we have had  devs rug project and made away with peoples hard earned.

So, a group of fellas came together to give back trust and build a sustainable community.

Kouta-kun inu is a deflationary Token/NFT projects that provides a decentralized financial asset which rewards users with 8% Bnb rewards 30 minutes directly to their wallets automatically.

The interest and safety of our investors is our top most priority. Thereby providing for them a top notch tokenomics.

Moreso, our NFT project provide our investors with passive income, in form of staking. Earning 2.5% of kouta-kun- Inu which can be converted to BNB when NFT is staked.

Building market place. There shall be our own NFT market place for the launch of our NFT.

Methodolgy

Diamond Hand Rescuer

Diamond Hand Rescuer (D H R) is a methodolgy used that implements a 25% tax fee on sellers, selling below the floor priced.

Thereby, introducing a burning mechanism where the funds received will use to buy NFTs in the floor price region and burning them to automatically boost the price and give our investors good returns.

Airdrop

1.5% of total airdrop will be given to holders of our tokens. Those who hold above 50$ worth of kouta-kun In will be eligible for this airdrop.

0.5% of the remaining airdrop will be used for giveaways and engagement activities

Burning

28% of token supply will be burned in tow batches to lower the total supply and increase price.

First burn, will take place 24hrs after launch.

Second burn will take place 3months after launch.
